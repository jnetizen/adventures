## SCENE BACKGROUNDS (6 images)

### PROLOGUE - Starting Line
Three young underwater adventurers at an underwater race starting line, bright coral arch with banner reading "GREAT RACE", excited crowd of colorful fish watching, three arguing crabs (the Crabby Brothers) on one side, two preening dolphins checking their reflections on the other side, crystal clear tropical water, sunbeams streaming down from surface, colorful coral and seaweed decorations.

Characters positioned separately (NOT overlapping, NOT touching), feet visible on sandy ocean floor. Pixar-adjacent storybook style, soft painterly rendering, bright and colorful, exciting race atmosphere.

### SCENE 1 - Bubble Geyser Field
Three young underwater adventurers swimming through a field of bubble geysers, giant bubbles shooting up from the ocean floor, some bubbles are huge and rainbow-tinted, playful chaotic energy, in the background the Crabby Brothers are arguing and the Showoff Dolphins got blasted sideways by a bubble, bright turquoise water, coral formations around the edges.

Pixar-adjacent storybook style, soft painterly rendering, bright and colorful, action and movement.

### SCENE 2 - Rainbow Whirlpools
Three young underwater adventurers approaching three huge swirling whirlpools made of bright rainbow-colored water - one PINK, one BLUE, one GOLD - spinning with sparkles and magical light, a wooden sign says "SHORTCUT - IF YOU DARE!", in the background the Showoff Dolphins are admiring their reflections in the sparkly water, bright cheerful underwater scene.

Pixar-adjacent storybook style, soft painterly rendering, magical and colorful, swirling rainbow effects.

### SCENE 3 - Grumpy Giant's Garden
Three young underwater adventurers at the entrance to a huge beautiful underwater garden with giant seaweed tall as trees, enormous colorful anemones, giant clams, in the CENTER a MASSIVE grumpy purple octopus sits with arms crossed blocking the path, a funny sign on his belly says "NO RACING", in the background the Crabby Brothers hide behind a rock and the Dolphins are covered in ink, lush colorful garden setting.

Pixar-adjacent storybook style, soft painterly rendering, lush garden colors, the octopus should look grumpy but not scary.

### SCENE 4 - Rainbow Reef Finale
Three young underwater adventurers arriving at the magnificent RAINBOW REEF, water glowing pink gold and blue, a beautiful coral arch marks the finish line, beneath it sits a giant treasure chest covered in sparkling gems and jewels, magical golden light emanating from the chest, in the far background the Showoff Dolphins are swimming fast trying to catch up, triumphant exciting atmosphere.

Pixar-adjacent storybook style, soft painterly rendering, magical glowing rainbow colors, triumphant finale feeling.

### ENDING - Coronation
Three young underwater adventurers wearing golden crowns, floating proudly in front of the open treasure chest at Rainbow Reef, magical sparkles all around them, the Crabby Brothers bowing (still arguing), the Showoff Dolphins clapping while checking their reflections, crowds of colorful fish cheering, a friendly purple octopus crying happy tears in the background, a tiny fish and seahorse cheering in the front row, royal celebration atmosphere, rainbow-colored water glowing.

Pixar-adjacent storybook style, soft painterly rendering, joyful celebration, royal and magical.
